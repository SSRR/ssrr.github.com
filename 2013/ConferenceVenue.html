Conference Venue

SSRR 2013 will be held at the University of Linköping, Linköping, Sweden.

Getting There and Away

Linköping has its own Linköping City airport, which can be reached from Amsterdam and Copenhagen by KLM and Lufthansa, respectively. The nearest Ryanair airport is the Skavsta Airport, from which one can reach Linköping around an hour by taking the Flygbussarna Shuttle busses. Furthermore, there is the Stockholm airport (which actually is located in Arlanda). From Arlanda there are direct train connections to Linköping taking 2-3 hours.

Getting Around

Taxi service within Linköping costs around 150 SEK, for example, between the University and City center. There is a very well working transportation system but it is not possible to pay on the bus. One needs to buy a ticket in advanced that can be refilled at the university or train station. More information will follow.

Visa information

SSRR 2013 provides official letters of invitation to persons who have fully registered for the conference and for which payment has been received. Please send an e-mail to alexander.kleiner AT liu.se. Note that per IEEE Policy, only registered, paid persons may receive an invitation letter to the conference. This letter does not guarantee that you will receive a visa. It is recommended that you start this process as soon as possible!
