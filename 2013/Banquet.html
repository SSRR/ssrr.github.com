Visualiseringscenter

Opened in 2010, the Norrköping Visualization Center in Norrköping hosts a variety of different activities, all connected to the visualization field. The starting point for the center is the visualization research conducted at Linköping University. Interactive exhibits and a 99 seat dome theater is the core of the public arena. The center also produces dome content (real time and pre-rendered) as well as interactive installations and exhibitions.
