Access

To Shin-Chitose (New Chitose, Sapporo)Airport

The nearest airport to Lake Toya is Shin(New) Chitose International Airport (CTS). There are many domestic fights connecting to Tokyo (Haneda, Narita), Osaka(Kansai, Itami, Kobe), Nagoya, and other cities in Japan. There are some international flights from/to Asian cities such as Seoul, Beijing, Shanghai, Taipei, Hong-Kong. Shin Chitose Airport is located about 50km south-east of the center of Sapporo city and it takes 37 minutes by airport shuttle trains.

From Airport to Toyako (Toyako Onsen)

There are no shuttle bus services directly connecting the airport and Lake Toya. You have to use trains and a bus / taxi.

  1. Move from International/Domestic Airport Building to New Chitose Airport Station of JR (Japan Railways) Hokkaido. The railway station is located on the first basement level of the airport buildings.
  2. Buy a train ticket for a limited express train to Toya Station. You can take any train at New Chitose Airport Station.
  3. Get off at the next station Minami-Chitose and change the trains that go to Toya.
  4. Get off at Toya Station. (about 80 minutes ride)
  5. Take a bus or a taxi to your hotel in Toyako Onsen (Hot springs). By taxi: 20 minutes and 2,000 JPY from the station.
